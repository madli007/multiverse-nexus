# Product concept

Multiverse Nexus is a cosmic observation platform suspended outside normal space and time.

At its centre is a massive multiversal nexus:

* a bright dimensional core,
* rotating mechanical and energy rings,
* branching timeline streams,
* reality fragments,
* luminous pathways,
* orbiting dimensional spheres.

Each dimensional sphere represents an established MCU universe, realm, location, or timeline concept.

The user should be able to:

* observe the complete multiverse,
* hover over dimensional worlds,
* select one,
* smoothly travel toward it,
* view a short cinematic representation,
* trigger a nexus event,
* watch realities destabilize,
* restore or isolate a timeline,
* return to the central nexus.

The application should feel like an interactive cinematic installation, not like a normal information website.

# Visual hierarchy

The scene should have three major visual layers.

## Layer 1: Central Nexus

The central nexus is always the dominant focal point.

It should include:

* a brilliant energy core,
* concentric mechanical rings,
* internal rotating structures,
* temporal energy pulses,
* dimensional distortion,
* light beams,
* branching pathways,
* orbiting particles,
* subtle reality fractures.

It must feel active and powerful even when the user does nothing.

## Layer 2: MCU Realities and Dimensions

Several large dimensional spheres orbit around the nexus.

Each sphere should have a clearly different identity.

Possible initial set:

* Sacred Timeline / Earth-616,
* Earth-838,
* the TVA,
* the Void,
* the Quantum Realm,
* the Dark Dimension,
* a *What If...?*-style branched reality,
* an incursion-affected collapsing universe.

Not every concept needs to be represented as a literal planet.

For example:

* the TVA may appear as a retro-futuristic temporal station outside the timeline,
* the Void may appear as a broken wasteland surrounded by timeline debris,
* the Quantum Realm may appear as a layered microscopic ecosystem,
* the Dark Dimension may appear as a folded kaleidoscopic reality,
* Earth-838 may appear as a clean advanced alternate Earth,
* the Sacred Timeline may appear as a protected golden reality connected directly to the nexus.

The visuals should be inspired by their MCU appearances, but created procedurally rather than copied from film assets.

## Layer 3: Timeline Connections

Reality spheres are connected to the nexus through:

* glowing timeline paths,
* moving particles,
* energy ribbons,
* branching streams,
* unstable fractures,
* incursion links.

Connections must communicate state.

Examples:

* stable: smooth blue-gold flow,
* branching: multiple diverging strands,
* unstable: flickering violet-orange energy,
* critical: fractured red pulses,
* isolated: dim disconnected path,
* collapsed: debris and fading particles.
